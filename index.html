About ME

"What we play is life." - Louis Armstrong

Living and working in San Francisco.

Marcus was born and raised on the coast of southern Maine (a few hours north of Boston, Massachusettes).


Everyday he explored, whether through the pages of books or the vast forests around him.


He spent Maine's harsh winters testing the limits of icy ponds. During the summers he explored rocky beaches, swam in the cold waves of the atlantic, and caught crabs in shallow tidal pools. He learned adventure and discovery was the key ingredient to understanding how the world works.


His desire to understand the world led him to The Gallatin School of Individualized Study at New York University where he studied interesections between, business, economics, technology, psychology, and innovation.


He became an entrepreneur to understand how the world connects outside academia.


He dove into understanding the intersection between technology and innovation. Writing code in web development gave him the power to build experiences and turn ideas into products people value.


Today he is a freelance full-stack developer and startup entrepreneur driven to make the world better.
