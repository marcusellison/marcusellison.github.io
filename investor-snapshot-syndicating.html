    About the Product

    Description

    Street lighting is 40% of a city’s overall electricity costs. Gridcomm provides the most reliable two-way data network over existing city infrastructure. This allows GridComm to connect streetlights with IoT sensors and provide street light control software.

    We allow Target Customer to:

    • Monitor streetlight infrastructure in real time
    • Automatically dim lights
    • Predict maintenance

    About the Market

    Addressable Market

    There are 1 billion streetlights in the world. GridComm revenue is S$100 per installed light with a 65% margin. GridComm’s target market is S$100 billion.

    Business Model

    GridComm charges an average upfront fee of $13 million USD per city. GridComm also charges an average of S$100,000/month/per city to use street light control software. (GridComm doesn’t charge the first year for software SAAS)

    Value

    GridComm saves the average city S$10 million per year. Cities recoup investment in 16 months.
